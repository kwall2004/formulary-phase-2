Requesting to Change your PCP

When you want to change your PCP, you can submit a request through the HPM Member Portal. You can search for a new PCP by name, or you can search by county, gender, city and hospital affiliations. The more options that you select will narrow your search results.

*If you leave the Specialty field blank, only PCP providers will appear in your search.

  1. Click the Change my PCP bar on the left side of the screen.

  2. Click the Language drop-down list and pick the language you want your PCP to speak.

  3. Click the County drop-down list and select the click the county that you live in.

  4. Click the Gender drop-down list and select the gender of the PCP you want.
    *If you leave this field blank, you will find male and female PCP's.

  5. Click the City drop-down list and click the city that you live in.
    *If you leave this field blank, you increase the number of PCP's that you can choose from.

  6. Click the Show all providers accepting new members check box.

  7. Click Search.
    A list of providers appears in the list box.

  8. Click the PCP that you want to switch to.
    Information about the PCP you select appears below the list of PCP's. This includes the PCP's name, address, office hours (if available) and any special messages from the PCP. Messages can be information such as the ages the PCP accepts.

  9. Scroll down to the bottom of the screen.

  10. Click the Effective Date drop-down list.

  11. From the list, click the date you want the change to be effective.

  12. Click the Change My PCP button.

  13. A message box opens informing you that the change has been submitted. Click OK.
