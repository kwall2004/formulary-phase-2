Changing your Address or Phone Number

The Member Profile section stores your name and address information and your phone number and email address. If you move, or get a new phone number or email address, you can change this information on the My Profile page.

  1. Click the My Profile bar on the left side of the screen.

  2. If you want to change your phone number, click in the Home, Work or Cell field (depending on which number you want to change) and remove what is currently there. You can do this by clicking in the front of the number and pressing [Delete] on you keyboard.

  3. Type the correct phone number.

  4. Click the Save Profile button.

  5. A message box opens stating your request has been submitted. You will be notified through email when it is complete. Click OK.
