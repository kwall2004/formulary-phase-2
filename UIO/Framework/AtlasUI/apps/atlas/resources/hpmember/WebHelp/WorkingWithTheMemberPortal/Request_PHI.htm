Requesting PHI

You can begin the process to request to receive your Protected Health Information (PHI) from the Member Portal. There are two forms that you need to fill out, sign and mail back to the following address.

Health Plan of Michigan

Attn:  Privacy Officer

777 Woodward Avenue

Suite 600

Detroit, MI  48226

 

  1. To access the forms, click the links at the bottom of the screen.
    The form opens in an Adobe Reader window. You can type your answers directly in to Adobe.

  2. Type your answers directly into the form. Click in each field and type the necessary response.

  3. When you finish typing your data in to the form, you need to print it, sign it and mail to the health plan. To print the form, click the File menu.

  4. Click the Print... command.

  5. Click the Print button.
