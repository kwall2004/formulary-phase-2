Retrieving a Lost Password

If you forget your password to the HPM Member Portal, you can reset it by clicking the Forgot Password link on the Login page. In order to reset your password you need the answers to the security questions you chose when you first activated your account.

  1. Click the Forgot password link on the login screen.
    The Forgot Password screen opens.

  2. Click in the Medicaid ID field and enter your Medicaid identification number.

  3. Press [Tab].
    Your security questions appear    .

  4. In the space provided, type your answer to the first question.

  5. Press [Tab] to move to the next question.

  6. In the space provided, type your answer to second question.

  7. Click the Reset password button.

  8. A message box opens stating that your password has been reset and sent to your email address. Click OK.
    You are directed back to the Login page. As soon as you receive your new password, you can log in.
