Requesting a Handbook

You can request that a copy of the Health Plan of Michigan Member Handbook be sent to your home.

  1. Click the Request Handbook menu bar on the left side of the screen.

  2. At the bottom of the page, the question "Are you sure you want to request a Health Plan of Michigan Member Handbook?" If you do, click Yes.
    A message stating that your request for a member handbook has been submitted successfully. Please wait at least two weeks to receive your new handbook in the mail.
